Game instructions:
Player Colors go as follows,
	Player 1 - Red
	Player 2 - Blue
	Player 3 - Green
	Player 4 - Purple
Click on your own color's planets to move troops
Each line indicates a planet that can be attacked on the opposing end of it.
Right clicking deselects a selected planet.
Left clicking on a planet with a line on it after selecting one of your own planets will transfer troops to the other.
Capture all the planets to win!
